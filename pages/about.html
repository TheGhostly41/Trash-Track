About us

TrashTrack is a smart-waste disposal tool designated to help users properly manage their trash.
By selecting a waste type such as, plastic, food scraps, napkins, users are instantly guided to
the nearest trash bin accordingly to the user's waste. This tool helps finding trash bins convenient
and helps promote sustainable eco-friendly practices by ensuring items are sorted properly and in the
right place. TrashTrack's encourages recycling, and keeps the community clean.
