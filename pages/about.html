About us

TrashTrack is a smart waste-disposal tool designed to help users manage their trash efficiently. By selecting
a waste type—such as plastic, food scraps, or napkins—users are instantly guided to the nearest appropriate
trash bin. TrashTrack makes finding bins convenient while promoting sustainable, eco-friendly practices by
ensuring items are properly sorted. It encourages recycling and helps keep communities clean.
